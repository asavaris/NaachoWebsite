About
Naa•cho
1. v. int. pl. imp. (Hindi): Dance.
2. n. Princeton's premiere Indian Dance Troupe, performing Hindi/Bollywood, Garba/Raas, Bhangra, Classical, Folk, and Fusion numbers.
3. n. Misspelling of nacho, a small, often triangular piece of tortilla topped with cheese or salsa.

Naacho is devoted to the beautiful and expressive art of Indian dance. Our group seeks to promote South Asian culture through displaying a variety of traditional and contemporary Indian dance styles including: Bollywood, folk, garba/raas, bhangra, and classical forms. By performing in as many venues as possible, Naacho attempts to increase awareness of the vast and diverse cultures that originate from South Asia. In addition to dancing, we make every effort to involve ourselves with community service ranging from teaching Indian dance to donating funds to causes that benefit the South Asian community worldwide.
